Membership Contract

I, [name], am entering a contract with the Manchester Makerspace.

&nbsp;

  1. I recognize that the nature of the Manchester Makerspace includes some dangerous tools and activities. I will adhere to the appropriate safety behavior for myself and ensure any guests I bring to the Manchester Makerspace also conduct themselves in a safe manner.
  2. I will adhere to the term limits, guest limits, and attendance limits (for group memberships) without trying to bypass the rules or any technical implementations designed to enforce them.
  3. I have read, and I agree to abide by, the Code of Conduct of the Manchester Makerspace when at the Makerspace or at any event associated with the Manchester Makerspace. The current version of the Code of Conduct is published online at: http://www.manchestermakerspace.org/code-of-conduct 
  4. Changes made to the Code of Conduct by a vote of the Board of Directors will be published for all members to see.
  5. I understand that the Board of Directors, in pursuit of maintaining a safe environment for the Manchester Makerspace community, may request additional identification information for the purposes of conducting a background check. The board reserves the right to dismiss or reject membership based on the results of the background check at any time.
  6. I understand that memberships are non-refundable.
  7. Minors, meaning those under 18 years old, must be accompanied by a parent or legal guardian at all times.
  8. I will indemnify and hold Manchester Makerspace harmless from any and all liability, loss, injury, damages, costs and expenses, including reasonable counsel fees, resulting from any injuries to myself or my guests at the Manchester Makerspace.  I and my guests will be responsible to adhere to all rules of safety at Manchester Makerspace.

        This agreement is binding for membership beginning on [today], and for all future renewals or periods of membership I have with the Manchester Makerspace.
